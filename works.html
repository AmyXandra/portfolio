    Ogwugo Admin Dashboard

    Ogwugo is an online store that orders delivery service for its orders. To manage their growing number of orders, the idea of the admin dashboard came up.

    Ogwugo admin dashboard helps to effectively manage orders. Orders can come in through the ogwugo app and the employees can create orders too. Employees assign orders to the *available* riders and after the orders are completed, they remit the orders.

    Through the dashboard, the company owner can monitor all activities or handle all delivery activities more effectively.


    Client

    Ogwugo Limited


    Creative Field / Stack

    React JS, Redux JS, Postman, UX/UI Design
