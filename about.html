Hi! I’m Amy.

Principally, I’m a front-end developer and graphics designer. I have a remarkable sense for aesthetics and art which allows me to create amazing web interfaces having fun with my favourite graphical tools and codes. I stay true to my code and keep an open mind to continue learning and developing me, staying relevant goes beyond being on top which is why adaptation was a necessary skill I had to take on.

I studied Electronic Engineering at the University of Nigeria Nsukka and earned a bachelor’s degree in this field, even before going pro I have been actively involved in a lot of electronic engineering projects.

I’m a great lover of humanity, and I’ve always wanted to help out any little way I can. Today, I’m glad to be part of a profession that can create a significant impact on the present day generation and more to come.

I’m drop dead serious when it comes to my work, yet, it doesn’t stop me from grabbing a cup of cappuccino from my favourite coffee mug during work or jamming to rock music. I love travelling, cooking and spending quality time with my friends and loved ones.
